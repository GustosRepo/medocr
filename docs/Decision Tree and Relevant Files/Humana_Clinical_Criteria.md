## KEY CHARACTERISTIC: EDS + SYMPTOMS REQUIRED

Humana requires **Excessive Daytime Sleepiness (EDS)** plus at least one additional symptom.
Unlike some payers, comorbidities alone qualify for PSG but NOT for HST.

---

## 1. HOME SLEEP TESTING (HST) CRITERIA

### Approved When ALL of the Following:

**A. Symptoms Present:**
- Excessive daytime sleepiness (EDS) AND at least ONE of:
  - ESS score >=10
  - Excessive sleepiness while driving
  - Loud/intense snoring
  - Witnessed nocturnal apnea, choking and/or gasping

**B. Absence of Comorbid Conditions** (that reduce HST accuracy):

| Category | Contraindication |
|----------|------------------|
| **Pulmonary** | COPD with FEV1 <60%, home oxygen use, or CO2 >=45 mmHg |
| **Pulmonary** | Nocturnal or uncontrolled asthma |
| **Neuromuscular** | ALS, MS, myotonic dystrophy, Parkinson's, stroke with respiratory effects, spina bifida, uncontrolled epilepsy |
| **Cardiac** | CHF (NYHA Class III/IV or LVEF <45%) |
| **Cardiac** | Pulmonary hypertension |
| **Cardiac** | Uncontrolled cardiac arrhythmia |

**C. Equipment Requirements:**
- Type II, Type III, or Type IV device (minimum 3 channels: heart rate, O2 sat, respiratory analysis)
- PAT (Peripheral Artery Tonometry) devices also accepted

**D. Patient Capability:**
- Individual or caregiver has appropriate cognitive function, dexterity and mobility to use equipment safely at home

**E. Evaluation:**
- Results must be evaluated by a qualified physician

---

## 2. REPEAT HST CRITERIA

### Approved When ANY of the Following:

- Evaluation following adjustment of an oral appliance prescribed for OSA
- Evaluation following surgery for OSA
- Reassessment after significant weight change (>=10% body weight) with persistent OSA symptoms

---

## 3. FACILITY-BASED PSG CRITERIA

### Approved When BOTH:

**A. Symptoms Present:**
- EDS AND at least ONE of:
  - ESS score >=10
  - Excessive sleepiness while driving
  - Loud/intense snoring
  - Witnessed nocturnal apnea, choking and/or gasping

**B. Individual Presents With ANY of:**

| Indication | Details |
|------------|---------|
| **HGNS Planning** | Diagnostic testing prior to planned hypoglossal nerve stimulator implantation |
| **Central Sleep Apnea** | History/features of CSA including Cheyne-Stokes, high-altitude, or opioid-induced (>=2 months) |
| **Narcolepsy** | Moderate-severe daytime sleepiness with cataplexy, hypnagogic hallucinations, or sleep paralysis (when MSLT planned) |
| **OHS** | BMI >30 with alveolar hypoventilation (PaCO2 >45 mmHg) not explained by other conditions |
| **TBI** | History of traumatic brain injury with EDS |
| **Idiopathic Hypersomnia** | Difficult morning awakening, constant somnolence, prolonged night sleep, or sleep drunkenness (when MSLT planned) |
| **Cognitive/Physical** | Individual or caregiver lacks cognitive function, dexterity, or mobility for home equipment |
| **Mission Critical** | Airline pilots, bus drivers, military personnel, truck drivers |
| **Pulmonary Disease** | COPD with FEV1 <60%, home O2, CO2 >=45; or nocturnal/uncontrolled asthma |
| **Neuromuscular** | ALS, MS, myotonic dystrophy, Parkinson's, stroke with respiratory effects, spina bifida, uncontrolled epilepsy |
| **Parasomnias** | Unusual or atypical due to duration, frequency, or timing (confusional arousals, nocturnal seizures, REM sleep behavior disorder) |
| **Seizure-Related** | Paroxysmal arousals or sleep disruptions thought to be seizure related |
| **PLMD** | Periodic limb movement disorder (involuntary leg jerking causing EDS) |
| **Cardiac Disease** | CHF (NYHA III/IV or LVEF <45%), pulmonary hypertension, or uncontrolled arrhythmia |

**Note:** PLMD is NOT the same as RLS. PLMD involves involuntary repetitive limb movements during sleep.

---

## 4. REPEAT FACILITY-BASED PSG CRITERIA

### Approved When ANY of the Following:

- Diagnostic testing prior to planned HGNS implantation
- Individual lacks cognitive function, dexterity, or mobility for home equipment
- Reassessment after significant weight change (>=10%) with persistent symptoms
- Previous home test was indeterminate or technically inadequate with high pretest probability
- Post-HGNS implantation:
  - Titrating device parameters (~1 month after implant)
  - Substantial weight gain (>=10%) with return of OSA symptoms

---

## 5. PAP TITRATION STUDY CRITERIA

### Approved When BOTH:

**A. Diagnosis Confirmed:**
- Conclusive diagnosis of OSA documented by sleep study

**B. AND ANY of the Following:**

| Indication | Details |
|------------|---------|
| **APAP Failure** | Failure of auto-titrating PAP treatment trial |
| **CSA** | History/features of central sleep apnea |
| **OHS** | BMI >30 with alveolar hypoventilation (PaCO2 >45 mmHg) |
| **Cognitive/Physical** | Individual lacks cognitive function, dexterity, mobility for home |
| **Comorbidities** | Conditions that degrade home test accuracy (pulmonary, neuromuscular, cardiac) |
| **Compliance Failure** | OSA symptoms persist despite documented PAP compliance attempts with provider support |

---

## 6. MSLT / MWT CRITERIA

### Approved When ALL of the Following:

1. Assessment of treatment response OR presence of narcolepsy features:
   - Cataplexy
   - EDS/hypersomnia
   - Hypnagogic hallucinations
   - Sleep paralysis

2. Testing consists of nap opportunities at 2-hour intervals (initial nap within 90 minutes to 3 hours after nocturnal PSG)

3. Testing performed daytime **immediately following negative nocturnal PSG** where:
   - Minimum 6 hours sleep achieved
   - Sleep apnea NOT diagnosed

## 7. COVERAGE LIMITATIONS (NOT COVERED)

### Sleep Studies NOT Covered For:

| Indication | Reason |
|------------|--------|
| Circadian rhythm disorders | Not covered |
| Common parasomnias | Bruxism, enuresis, nightmares, sleep talking |
| Insomnia | Not covered |
| Restless legs syndrome (RLS) | Not covered (RLS != PLMD) |

### Technologies/Methods NOT Covered:

| Technology | Reason |
|------------|--------|
| AI sleep testing | Experimental/investigational |
| PAP NAP testing | Experimental/investigational |
| UARS titration | Upper airway resistance syndrome |
| Prescreening devices | Pre-test probability devices |
| Standalone actigraphy | Experimental/investigational |
| OTC devices | Generally excluded by certificate |

### MSLT/MWT NOT Covered When:

- Following a POSITIVE PSG (sleep apnea diagnosed)
- Single nap studies for any sleep disorder
- Unattended or home MSLT

## 12. DECISION TREE INTEGRATION

### For HST Authorization:
```
CHECK: EDS + at least one symptom?
|-- NO -> DENY
+-- YES -> CHECK: Any HST contraindications (comorbids)?
    |-- YES -> Route to PSG
    +-- NO -> CHECK: Patient/caregiver capable?
        |-- NO -> Route to PSG
        +-- YES -> APPROVE HST
```

### For PSG Authorization:
```
CHECK: EDS + at least one symptom?
|-- NO -> DENY
+-- YES -> CHECK: Meets PSG indication criteria?
    |-- YES -> APPROVE PSG
    +-- NO -> Route to HST first
```

### For PAP Titration:
```
CHECK: Confirmed OSA diagnosis?
|-- NO -> DENY (need diagnostic first)
+-- YES -> CHECK: Meets titration criteria?
    |-- YES -> APPROVE titration
    +-- NO -> Try APAP first
```

## 14. APPENDIX: NYHA Classification

| Class | Symptoms |
|-------|----------|
| I (Mild) | Cardiac disease, no limitation on physical activity |
| II (Mild) | Slight limitation; ordinary activity causes symptoms |
| III (Moderate) | Marked limitation; less than ordinary activity causes symptoms |
| IV (Severe) | Unable to carry on any physical activity without discomfort |

---

## 15. APPENDIX: Epworth Sleepiness Scale (ESS)

Score each situation 0-3:
- 0 = Would never doze
- 1 = Slight chance of dozing
- 2 = Moderate chance
- 3 = High chance

**Situations:**
- Sitting and reading
- Watching TV
- Sitting inactive in public place
- Passenger in car
- Lying down to rest in afternoon
- Sitting talking to someone
- Sitting after lunch without alcohol
- In a car, stopped for minutes in traffic

**Total Score 0-24:**
- ESS >=10 = Abnormal, supports EDS
